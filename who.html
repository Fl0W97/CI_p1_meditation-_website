The author of the book 'Search Inside Yourself'

Certainly! Chade-Meng Tan, often referred to as Meng, is not your typical tech guru. He's like the Zen master who found his way into the heart of Silicon Valley. Meng started his journey at Google as an engineer but soon became known as Google's "Jolly Good Fellow" for his quirky and uplifting personality.


He created the Search Inside Yourself (SIY) program, blending meditation with emotional intelligence training. Meng's book "Search Inside Yourself" shares his insights in a delightful mix of anecdotes, exercises, and research. Beyond Google, Meng travels the world spreading joy and compassion, advocating that happiness is a skill we can cultivate. His approach is down-to-earth and inclusive, making complex concepts feel light and approachable. Meng reminds us that success and happiness are intertwined, and the journey inward is just as important as the one outward.
